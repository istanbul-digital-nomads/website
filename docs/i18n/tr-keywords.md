## Notes

- Always pair "ikamet" with "izni" once in the document, then can shorten.
- Local audiences search both "dijital göçebe" and "remote worker İstanbul" - cover both.
- For procedural visa content prefer formal `siz`; for personal-story blog posts use informal `sen`. Stay consistent inside a piece.
- Currency: keep TL/USD figures from the English source. Format numbers Turkish-style (1.000 thousands separator, 1,5 decimal comma) and put TL/USD after the number.
- Loanwords kept as-is (widely understood): wifi, Airbnb, IMEI, fiber, cappuccino, cortado. Lokanta and aidat stay in Turkish as proper terms.

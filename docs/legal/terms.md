## 6. Plans: hosting, joining, money

### What a "plan" is

A plan is a member-hosted meetup at a specific date, time, and place in
Istanbul. Plans have one or more stops. Plans are either **budget plans**
(no money flows through the platform, the budget field is informational
only) or **ticketed plans** (the host has set an entry fee per attendee,
which is collected by us and paid out to them).

### Who can host

Anyone with a Red badge can host a budget plan. Only Blue or Gold verified
`local_guide`, `tour_guide`, and `agent` members can host ticketed plans.
Hosting a ticketed plan also requires either a free monthly slot (1 paid
plan per month) or an active paid subscription tier.

### Joining a plan

When you join a plan you're committing to show up at the listed start time.
You agree to honor the host's plan-level guidelines (vibe, language,
difficulty, what to bring). You agree to the per-vibe disclaimer attached
to the plan (see [Plan Disclaimers](plan-disclaimers.md)).

### Paying for a ticketed plan

When you join a ticketed plan, you pay the entry fee at the time of
booking. The payment goes to **iyzico** (our Turkish payment processor; for
non-Turkish-resident hosts the fallback is **Stripe Connect**). We hold the
gross ticket price in escrow until the plan completes.

We deduct:
- A **10% platform fee** on the gross.
- The processor's fee (typically **~2.9%**).

The host receives the remainder.

### Payout to hosts

We release the host's share **7 days after the plan's scheduled end time**,
provided no attendee has filed a dispute. If a dispute is filed, we hold
the funds until the dispute is resolved (usually within 14 days). Funds are
paid out to the bank account the host registered during their Blue-badge
KYC. We support TRY payouts via iyzico for Turkish-resident hosts and
[CURRENCY TBD] payouts via Stripe Connect for everyone else.

### Refund policy for attendees

- **24+ hours before the plan starts:** full refund.
- **Between 24 hours and the start time:** no refund unless the host
  agrees to one or unless the host cancels.
- **No-show:** no refund. We log the no-show against your account (see
  [Community Guidelines](community-guidelines.md) - three strikes in 90
  days suspends).
- **Host cancels:** full refund to all attendees. Repeated host
  cancellations may downgrade the host's badge or end their subscription.
- **Force majeure** (weather closes ferries, transit strike, terror event,
  pandemic restriction): we will work with the host and attendees to
  reschedule or refund. We don't promise an automatic refund for force
  majeure but we promise to be reasonable.

### Disputes

If you believe you didn't get what was advertised, file a dispute through
the plan page within 7 days of the plan's scheduled end. We review
disputes within 14 days. Possible outcomes: full refund, partial refund,
no refund, host warning, host badge downgrade. The decision is final
unless new evidence emerges.

## What is Istanbul Nomads?

We're a community for people working remotely from Istanbul - nomads passing through, remote workers settling in, and the local guides and helpers who make landing here easier. The site does three things: it shows you who's around, it lets you share and join casual plans, and it points you to the guides and services you'll actually need.

It's not a feed to scroll. It's a way to get off the laptop and meet people in real places.

## Your first steps

1. **Make a profile.** Takes about two minutes. Pick your neighborhood, say what you're working on, add a few skills and interests. The more you fill in, the easier it is for people to find common ground with you. You can edit any section later from your [dashboard](/dashboard/profile).
2. **See who's around.** Browse the [members directory](/members) by neighborhood or role. Everyone listed chose to be there.
3. **Check today's plans.** Open [plans](/plans) to see cowork sessions, dinners, and walks happening today. Some are free, some have a small entry fee. Tap to join.
4. **Just show up.** The app gets you in the door. The rest happens in person.

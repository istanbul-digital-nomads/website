## Why verification exists

Verification builds trust between people who haven't met yet. It tells you whether someone confirmed their identity, and it's what lets guides charge for plans. You don't need to be verified to join free plans or be in the directory - it mainly matters if you want to host paid plans or offer paperwork services.

## The three levels

| Badge | Level | What it means | What it unlocks |
|---|---|---|---|
| ● | **Basic** | Email and Telegram confirmed. Every member starts here. | Join free budget plans, appear in the directory. |
| ✓ | **Verified** | ID document and selfie checked. | Host paid (ticketed) plans, list paperwork services. |
| ★ | **Trusted** | Met an organizer in person in Istanbul. | The highest trust signal on your profile. |

## How to get verified

Head to your [dashboard](/dashboard/verify) and start a request. You'll submit what the level needs - ID and a selfie for Verified, an in-person meet for Trusted. Requests are reviewed by an organizer, and your badge updates once you're approved.

Verification is only offered where it's relevant - if you're a nomad who just wants to join plans, you won't be nudged to verify.

> **Heads up:** We verify identities, but a badge isn't a personal endorsement. Always use your own judgment when meeting someone or paying for something. See [trust and safety](/help/trust-and-safety).

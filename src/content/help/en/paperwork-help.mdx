## What this is

Turkish bureaucracy can be slow and confusing in a language you don't speak yet. The [paperwork directory](/paperwork) lists independent agents who help with the common things nomads need - residence permits (ikamet), tax numbers, bank accounts, notary work, and more.

## What agents help with

- **Residence permit / ikamet** - applications, appointments, renewals
- **Visa** questions and support
- **Bank account** setup
- **Tax number** registration
- **Notary** and document work
- **Address registration** and other official errands

## How it works

1. **Browse** the [directory](/paperwork) by service type. Each agent lists what they handle, with clear pricing and what's included.
2. **Pick who fits** - compare experience, languages spoken, and what past nomads said. Every agent is verified before they can list.
3. **Reach out directly** to ask questions and arrange the work. You deal with them one-on-one, on your own terms.

## Important

Agents are independent service providers, not employees of Istanbul Nomads. We verify identities but we **don't give legal advice**. Always confirm details and pricing directly with the agent before you commit, and keep records of what you agree to.

Want to offer paperwork services yourself? You'll need to [get verified](/help/getting-verified) first.

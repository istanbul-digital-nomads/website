## When money is involved

Most plans are free to join. Money only comes up for **ticketed plans** - a guided walk, a workshop, a dinner with a fixed cost - where a [verified guide](/help/getting-verified) set an entry fee. You buy a ticket to reserve your spot.

## How your money stays safe

When you buy a ticket, we hold the money - the guide doesn't get it right away. It only releases after the plan happens and the dust settles, so you're covered if something falls through.

1. **You pay** for your ticket.
2. **We hold it** in escrow.
3. **The plan happens.**
4. **The guide is paid** after a 7-day holdback clears.

## What the guide actually receives

The price you see is the price you pay. Out of that, a **10% platform fee** and a **payment processing fee (around 2.9%)** are deducted before the guide's share. The guide keeps roughly **87%** of the ticket price. Nothing is added on top of the sticker price at checkout.

## Refunds and disputes

- **Cancel in time:** if you cancel before the plan's cutoff, your ticket is refunded.
- **Host cancels:** if the guide cancels, you're refunded.
- **Something went wrong?** You can open a dispute within 7 days of the plan. That freezes the payout while it's looked into.

> Payments are rolling out gradually. If checkout isn't live for a plan yet, you'll see a clear note and can arrange it with the host directly - we never show a fake payment screen.

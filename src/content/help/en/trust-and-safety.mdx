## How we keep it real

A few things keep the community trustworthy:

- **Verification.** Members can confirm their identity and earn a [badge](/help/getting-verified). Guides who charge for plans must be verified.
- **Public profiles are opt-in.** You only appear in the [directory](/members) if you choose to. You can hide yourself anytime from your [dashboard](/dashboard/profile).
- **Escrow on paid plans.** Money for ticketed plans is held until after the plan - see [payments and escrow](/help/payments-and-escrow).

## This isn't a dating app

Istanbul Nomads is for working, meeting, and building a community. It's not for romantic matching, and unsolicited DMs of that kind aren't welcome. Keep it respectful - treat people the way you'd want a stranger in a new city to treat you.

## Meeting people safely

- Meet in public places first - cafes, coworking spaces, daytime plans.
- A badge means an identity was checked, not that we vouch for someone personally. Use your own judgment.
- Tell a friend where you're going if you're meeting someone new one-on-one.
- For anything involving money, get the details in writing and never feel rushed.

## Reporting a problem

If someone makes you uncomfortable, breaks the spirit of the community, or you suspect a scam, reach out to the organizers on Telegram (linked in the footer). We'd much rather hear about it early.

> Our community guidelines and terms spell out the rules in full - you'll find them linked in the site footer.
